About

Notes are a way to record information, thoughts, ideas, or observations for later reference. They can be written down physically (like in notebooks) or stored digitally in apps or documents.

Types of Notes

Personal Notes: Journals, diaries, or quick thoughts. Academic Notes: Summaries, lecture notes, and study materials. Work Notes: Meeting minutes, task lists, or brainstorming ideas. Digital Notes: Stored in apps or devices for easy access.
